# Strategic Buckets (Reference)

## Bucket 1: Fast Cash / Quick Wins
- Goal: immediate value (2–3 weeks max)
- Traits: low risk, fast execution, clear monetization
- Allocation: 10–20%
- Rule: timeline < 4 weeks AND ROI > 5x AND risk < 30%

## Bucket 2: Autonomous SaaS (Core)
- Goal: recurring revenue with low-touch ops
- Traits: automated signup/billing, scalable delivery, 70%+ margin
- Allocation: 40–50%
- Rule: autonomous_readiness > 80% AND margin > 70% AND recurring_revenue

## Bucket 3: Core Platform (Infrastructure)
- Goal: enable multiple products, reduce duplication
- Traits: reuse potential (3+ projects), technical moat, long-term asset
- Allocation: 20–30%
- Rule: reuse_potential >= 3 OR enables_future_products

## Bucket 4: Big Bets / Options
- Goal: breakthrough opportunities
- Traits: high uncertainty, long timeline, high expected value
- Allocation: 10–20%
- Rule: expected_value > 10x AND probability < 30%
