## Sphere-Specific Checklists

### Career/Professional Sphere

**Force 1: New Entrants (Your Competition)**
- [ ] How many people can do your job?
- [ ] How long does it take to learn?
- [ ] What barriers protect you? (certifications, experience, network)
- [ ] Are there bootcamps/fast-track entry programs?

**Force 2: Substitutes (Alternatives to Your Services)**
- [ ] Can automation replace your role?
- [ ] Are there alternative career paths solving same problem?
- [ ] Can outsourcing/freelancing substitute?
- [ ] AI/tools threatening your position?

**Force 3: Buyer Power (Your Employers/Clients)**
- [ ] How many potential employers/clients?
- [ ] How easy is it for them to hire elsewhere?
- [ ] How price-sensitive are they?
- [ ] Can they develop the skills in-house?

**Force 4: Supplier Power (Your Vendors/Platforms)**
- [ ] Dependent on one platform (LinkedIn, specific tech)?
- [ ] Can they change terms unilaterally?
- [ ] How locked-in are you?
- [ ] Alternatives available?

**Force 5: Rivalry**
- [ ] How many competitors for your type of work?
- [ ] How differentiated are you?
- [ ] Price wars? Or premium positioning?
- [ ] How stable is the competition?

### Business/Entrepreneurial Sphere

**Force 1: New Entrants**
- [ ] Startup capital required? (High = barrier)
- [ ] Regulatory approvals needed?
- [ ] Economies of scale? (Your business size advantage?)
- [ ] Brand loyalty in market?
- [ ] Technology/IP patents?

**Force 2: Substitutes**
- [ ] Alternative solutions customers use?
- [ ] Switching costs to your solution?
- [ ] Price comparison (substitute vs. you)?
- [ ] Market trends favoring alternatives?

**Force 3: Buyer Power**
- [ ] Customer concentration? (Few big vs. many small?)
- [ ] How easy to switch to competitor?
- [ ] How price-sensitive?
- [ ] Information transparency?

**Force 4: Supplier Power**
- [ ] Key suppliers concentration?
- [ ] Unique inputs or easily replaceable?
- [ ] Forward integration risk? (Suppliers become competitors?)
- [ ] Alternative suppliers available?
