## Execution Protocol

**How to use Six Hats in consilium:**

1. **Auto-assign hats** based on specialist type (use table above)
2. **Present hat-specific question** from their perspective
3. **Ask user to provide or confirm their input**
4. **Record each recommendation** with hat color prefix
5. **Ask 1-2 specialists at a time** and adapt based on user response

**Prompt format for each specialist:**

```
{Specialist Name} ({Hat Color} Hat - {Perspective}), please share your view:
- {Hat-specific question}

Keep recommendations focused on your hat's perspective.
```

## Why Six Hats Works

**Problem:** Traditional group discussions mix perspectives (facts, emotions, risks) chaotically
**Solution:** Parallel thinking - everyone wears same hat at same time
**Benefit:** Clear, organized discussion without ego conflicts

**Research Evidence:**
- IBM: 75% reduction in meeting time for complex decisions
- NASA: Improved risk assessment accuracy by 40%
- Used in 60+ countries for strategic planning
