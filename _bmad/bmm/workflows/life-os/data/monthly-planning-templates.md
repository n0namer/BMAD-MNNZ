# Monthly Planning Templates

## Next Month Priorities Template

**Questions to ask (MONTH scope):**

1. **Top 3 focus areas:**
   "What are the 3 most important things to complete NEXT MONTH?"

2. **Why these 3:**
   "Why are these the priorities? (link to goals/milestones)"

3. **Realistic check:**
   "Given trends, are these achievable?"

4. **Success criteria:**
   "How will you know these are done?"

## Why Monthly is "Deeper Weekly"

- **Daily** = Optional standup (1-2 min)
- **Weekly** = PRIMARY substantive review (15-20 min)
- **Monthly** = "Deeper weekly" (30 min, weekly format + trend analysis)
- **Quarterly** = Strategic goal adjustment only (OKR review)

Monthly adds to weekly:
1. **Trend analysis** (comparing last 4 weeks)
2. **Alignment check** (are projects supporting goals?)
3. **Resource allocation review** (are you investing time wisely?)
