# Porter's Five Forces Analysis Template

## Overview

Porter's Five Forces is a framework for analyzing industry attractiveness and competitive intensity. It helps you understand the structural dynamics of your market and make strategic decisions.

**Use this template to:**
- Assess market attractiveness before entering a sphere (C/A/B/D blocks)
- Identify competitive threats and opportunities
- Inform strategy selection (attack, defend, differentiate, niche)
- Track market shifts quarterly or on major events

---

## The Five Forces

### Force 1: Threat of New Entrants

**Definition:** How easily can new competitors enter your market?

#### Scoring Questions (1-10 scale)

| Question | Scale 1 (Easy Entry) | Scale 10 (High Barriers) | Your Score |
|----------|-------------------|----------------------|------------|
| **Capital Requirements** | Low startup costs | Massive upfront investment | ___ |
| **Regulatory Barriers** | No regulations | Heavy licensing/compliance | ___ |
| **Economies of Scale** | Irrelevant in industry | Huge cost advantage for large players | ___ |
| **Brand Loyalty** | Switching to new brand = easy | Strong brand moat | ___ |
| **Access to Distribution** | Easy online/direct | Controlled by incumbents | ___ |
| **Technology/IP Protection** | Easy to copy | Strong patents/trade secrets | ___ |
| **Network Effects** | Weak or absent | Strong (increases value per user) | ___ |

**Force 1 Score** (average of above): ___/10

#### Interpretation

- **Score 8-10:** High barriers = Few new entrants = Good for incumbents
- **Score 5-7:** Moderate barriers = Selective entry = Mixed
- **Score 1-3:** Low barriers = Constant new entrants = Tough for incumbents

## Overall Industry Attractiveness Score

### Calculation

```
Overall Score = (Force1 + Force2 + Force3 + Force4 + Force5) / 5 = ___/10
```

### Interpretation Matrix

| Overall Score | Attractiveness | Recommendation | Strategic Posture |
|---------------|---|---|---|
| **8.0-10.0** | Highly Attractive | Invest aggressively | Growth/Attack |
| **6.5-7.9** | Attractive | Proceed with confidence | Growth with caution |
| **5.0-6.4** | Moderately Attractive | Selective entry | Niche/Differentiate |
| **3.5-4.9** | Challenging | Proceed carefully | Defend/Consolidate |
| **1.0-3.4** | Unattractive | Reconsider | Divest/Abandon |

### Force-Specific Strengths & Weaknesses

| Force | Score 8-10 | Score 5-7 | Score 1-3 |
|-------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **New Entrants** | Moat intact | Selective entry | Open door |
| **Substitutes** | Few options | Some competition | Many alternatives |
| **Buyer Power** | You control pricing | Negotiated | Buyers dictate |
| **Supplier Power** | Good terms | Fair dealing | Supplier squeeze |
| **Rivalry** | Weak competition | Moderate | Intense battles |
