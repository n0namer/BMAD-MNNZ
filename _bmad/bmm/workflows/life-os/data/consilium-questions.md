## Follow-Up Questions (All Modes)

**If unclear:**
1. Can you elaborate on that?
2. What evidence supports this view?
3. What assumptions are you making?

**If contradictory:**
1. How do you reconcile X with Y?
2. What's the priority if we must choose?
3. What compromise might work?

**If missing detail:**
1. What specific metrics/criteria?
2. What's the magnitude/impact?
3. What's the timeline/urgency?
